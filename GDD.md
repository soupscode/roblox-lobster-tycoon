## üéÆ EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Lobster Tycoon** is a tycoon game where players build a lobster farming empire, starting with a single bucket and a dream. Catch lobsters, breed increasingly bizarre mutations, sell them to questionable buyers, and eventually unlock the dark truth: the lobsters are becoming sentient. Will you side with humanity or join the Crustacean Revolution?

---

## üîÑ CORE GAMEPLAY LOOP

### Phase 1: CATCH
- **Starting Equipment:** Rusty net, a bucket, crushing student debt
- Players click to catch wild lobsters from the ocean
- Early game: Manual clicking (carpal tunnel speedrun)
- Mid game: Unlock "Lobster Traps" (passive income)
- Late game: "Industrial Vacuum Boat" that just *s u c c s* lobsters from the sea

**Funny Twist:** Sometimes you catch trash (boots, old phones, expired coupons, a very confused crab who apologizes profusely)

### Phase 2: BREED
- Place lobsters in tanks to breed
- Breeding mechanics:
  - **Normal Breeding:** Red Lobster + Red Lobster = More Red Lobsters (boring but reliable)
  - **Genetic Experimentation:** Mix different rarities for CHAOS
  - **Forbidden Breeding:** Feed lobsters weird items (radioactive waste, energy drinks, cursed amulets) to create mutants

**Breeding Cooldowns:**
- Common: 30 seconds
- Rare: 2 minutes
- Epic: 5 minutes
- Legendary: 15 minutes
- Cryptid: 1 hour (worth it)

### Phase 3: SELL
- Sell lobsters to various buyers:
  - **Local Restaurant:** Standard prices, reliable
  - **Fancy Hotel:** 2x price for rare+ lobsters
  - **Black Market:** 5x price but has a chance to "investigate" you
  - **Mad Scientist:** Buys mutants at insane prices, asks no questions
  - **Lobster Liberation Front:** Pays you to FREE lobsters (ironic capitalism)
  - **The Void:** Appears at midnight, buys anything, pays in "Void Credits" (special currency)

### Phase 4: UPGRADE
- Spend cash on:
  - Better tanks (capacity, breeding speed, decoration)
  - Automation (auto-catchers, breeding bots)
  - New areas (unlock new biomes with unique lobsters)
  - Research tree (genetic modifications, efficiency upgrades)
  - **Lobster Happiness Meter:** Happy lobsters breed faster (must add toys, music, tiny lobster gyms)

### Phase 5: REPEAT (but weirder)
- The loop accelerates
- Unlock increasingly absurd content
- The lobsters start... watching you...

## üìà PROGRESSION SYSTEMS

### üí∞ Currency Types
1. **Cash:** Basic currency from selling lobsters
2. **Lobster Bucks:** Premium currency (Robux or earned slowly in-game)
3. **Research Points:** Earned by breeding new mutations
4. **Void Credits:** Mysterious currency for endgame content
5. **Respect:** Earned from the Lobster Council (yes, they organize)

### ü¶û Lobster Rarity System

#### COMMON LOBSTERS (Gray)
- Red Lobster (Standard)
- Brown Lobster (Tastes like chicken)
- Rock Lobster (B-52s intensifies)

#### UNCOMMON LOBSTERS (Green)
- Blue Lobster (1/2,000,000 IRL, 1/10 here)
- Yellow Lobster (Lemon-scented)
- Speedy Lobster (Runs in circles, 2x breeding)

#### RARE LOBSTERS (Blue)
- Cotton Candy Lobster (Pink & blue swirl)
- Crystal Lobster (Translucent, very fancy)
- Reverse Lobster (Tail where head should be, screams softly)

#### EPIC LOBSTERS (Purple)
- **Party Lobster:** Plays music, increases happiness of nearby lobsters
- **Buff Lobster:** Has tiny muscles, intimidates predators
- **Disco Lobster:** Flashes rainbow colors, sells for 10x to nightclubs
- **Lawyer Lobster:** Wears tiny suit, reduces black market risk by 50%

#### LEGENDARY LOBSTERS (Gold)
- **Diamond Lobster:** Literally made of diamonds (how???)
- **Ghost Lobster:** Phases through walls, spooky
- **Time Lobster:** Ages backwards, never dies
- **Void Lobster:** Pure black, whispers forbidden knowledge
- **CEO Lobster:** Wears glasses, generates passive income

#### MYTHICAL/CRYPTID LOBSTERS (Rainbow/???)
- **Lobster God:** Radiates golden light, other lobsters worship it
- **Kaiju Lobster:** Grows to enormous size, occasionally destroys tanks
- **Quantum Lobster:** Exists in multiple tanks simultaneously
- **Lobster President:** Campaigns for lobster rights, starts the uprising
- **The Lobstrosity:** Stephen King reference, has "did-a-chick" sound effects

### üèÜ Prestige System: "REBIRTH INTO LOBSTER NIRVANA"

When players reach $1,000,000 they can "Prestige":
- **Lose:** All lobsters, tanks, and money
- **Keep:** Research upgrades, cosmetics, Void Credits
- **Gain:** 
  - 1x Prestige Point (spend on permanent bonuses)
  - "Reincarnated" prefix (your lobsters come back with past-life memories)
  - Access to "Enlightened Lobsters" (new tier with cosmic powers)
  - A tiny golden statue of your best lobster

**Prestige Bonuses:**
- Catch Speed +10%
- Breeding Speed +15%
- Rare Mutation Chance +5%
- Unlock "Auto-Sell" Feature
- Unlock "Lobster Daycare" (passive breeding while offline)
- **"LOBSTER WHISPERER":** Lobsters tell you secrets (flavor text, hints, occasionally lottery numbers)

### üî¨ Research Tree

**BIOLOGY BRANCH:**
- Faster Breeding
- Genetic Stability (fewer mutations)
- Genetic Chaos (MORE mutations)
- Cloning Technology
- "Playing God" (final upgrade, unlocks creation of custom lobsters)

**BUSINESS BRANCH:**
- Better Prices
- Buyer Relationships
- Marketing (attracts rare buyers)
- "Lobster Insurance" (death protection)
- "Ponzi Scheme" (passive income but occasionally FBI visits)

**INFRASTRUCTURE BRANCH:**
- Larger Tanks
- Tank Decorations
- Climate Control
- Underwater Castles (lobsters love this)
- "Lobster University" (they learn skills???)

**FORBIDDEN BRANCH:**
- Radioactive Waste Barrels (mutation chance +500%)
- Dark Rituals (summon Void Lobsters)
- Mind Control Devices (make lobsters work harder)
- "FREE WILL REMOVAL" (increases efficiency, decreases your humanity)
- **THE LOBSTER THRONE:** Final upgrade, you become their king

## üíé MONETIZATION STRATEGY

### Game Passes (Robux)
1. **"VIP Lobster License"** (399 Robux)
   - 2x catch speed
   - 1.5x sell prices
   - Exclusive "Golden Net" tool
   - VIP-only lobsters (Influencer Lobster, Yacht Lobster)

2. **"Genetic Madness Pass"** (299 Robux)
   - 3x mutation chance
   - Unlock forbidden breeding combinations
   - Access to "The Mutation Lab" area
   - Spawn with "Mad Scientist" coat

3. **"Infinite Lobsters"** (499 Robux)
   - No tank capacity limits
   - "Bag of Holding" tank (holds infinite lobsters in quantum space)
   - Warning: May cause lag and existential dread

4. **"Time Lord"** (199 Robux)
   - Breeding timers reduced by 50%
   - Unlock "Time Lobster" immediately
   - Your character ages backwards (cosmetic)

5. **"Lobster Uprising DLC"** (599 Robux)
   - Join the lobster revolution
   - Play as a lobster (limited movement, mostly vibes)
   - Unlock "Rebellion" questline
   - Your tycoon becomes a lobster democracy

### Robux Shop Items

**Cosmetics:**
- Lobster-themed clothing (claws, antenna headband, shell backpack)
- Tank decorations (tiny pirate ships, SpongeBob references, eldritch symbols)
- Emotes ("Lobster Dance", "Pinch", "Sideways Scuttle")
- Character skins (Fisherman, Scientist, Lobster in Human Suit)

**Boosts (Temporary):**
- 2x Cash (1 hour) - 50 Robux
- Guaranteed Rare+ (5 catches) - 75 Robux
- Instant Breeding (10 uses) - 100 Robux
- "Lobster Magnet" (auto-catch for 30 min) - 80 Robux

**Special Lobsters (Direct Purchase):**
- Baby versions of Legendary lobsters (cute, but not as powerful)
- Seasonal lobsters (Halloween Ghost Lobster, Christmas Present Lobster)
- Collab lobsters (if partnering with other games/brands)

**Gacha System: "MYSTERY LOBSTER CRATE"**
- 50 Robux per crate
- 70% Common, 20% Uncommon, 7% Rare, 2.5% Epic, 0.5% Legendary
- Guaranteed Epic after 50 crates (pity system)
- Animated opening sequence with dramatic music
- Flex your rare lobsters in trade menu

### Free-to-Play Friendly Elements
- **Daily Rewards:** Login streak (Day 7 = Rare Lobster Egg)
- **Achievements:** Complete tasks for Lobster Bucks
- **Watch Ads:** Optional, get small boosts (1.5x cash for 5 minutes)
- **Events:** Weekend events with free limited-edition lobsters
- **Trading System:** Players can trade lobsters (prevents pure pay-to-win)

## üé™ UNIQUE HOOKS & FUNNY FEATURES

### üèÅ LOBSTER RACING LEAGUE

**Unlocks at:** Level 15

**How it Works:**
- Build a racing track in your tycoon
- Enter your fastest lobsters (Speedy Lobster has advantage)
- Races are 30 seconds of chaos
- Lobsters can sabotage each other (oil slicks, pinches, emotional manipulation)
- Winners get trophies and prize money
- Losers get "Participated" ribbon (builds character)

**Racing Stats:**
- **Speed:** How fast they scuttle
- **Stamina:** Can they finish without napping?
- **Aggression:** Will they fight or flee?
- **Luck:** Sometimes a lobster just wins because destiny

**Special Racing Events:**
- Weekly Tournaments (top 3 get exclusive cosmetics)
- "Death Race" mode (lobsters respawn, it's fine)
- Team Races (3v3 lobster relay)
- **"LOBSTER GRAND PRIX":** Monthly event, winner gets Legendary Lobster Egg + title "Crustacean Champion"

### üé≠ RANDOM EVENTS

**THE LOBSTER UPRISING** (5% chance per hour)
- Your lobsters gain sentience temporarily

- Fail: Pay fine, temporary breeding ban

- Ascended lobsters become NPCs that give quests

**KRAKEN ATTACK** (0.5% chance per hour)

**CELEBRITY VISIT** (2% chance per hour)

## ü¶û CONCLUSION

**Lobster Tycoon** combines addictive tycoon gameplay with absurdist humor, creating a unique experience that's both relaxing and hilariously chaotic. The progression from humble fisherman to Lobster Overlord gives players a sense of accomplishment, while random events and mutations keep gameplay unpredictable.

The game's secret sauce is its tonal shift: start cute and silly, gradually introduce existential questions about lobster sentience, and culminate in players questioning if they're the villain. But hey, at least the lobsters are happy! (Are they?)

**Core Appeal:**
- Satisfying progression loops
- Collectible lobsters (gotta catch 'em all)
- Humor that appeals to kids AND adults
- Social features (trading, racing, showing off)
- Depth beyond typical tycoon games

**Why it works:**
- Lobsters are inherently funny
- Mix of casual and hardcore content
- Respectful monetization (no aggressive pay-to-win)
- Meme potential (Lobster Uprising will trend on TikTok)
