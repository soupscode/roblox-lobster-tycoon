## Competitor Analysis

### Direct Competitors (Fishing/Ocean Tycoons):

#### 1. **Fishing Simulator**
- **Developer:** Cloud Entertainment
- **Visits:** 1B+
- **Strengths:** Massive content, established playerbase, realistic fishing mechanics
- **Weaknesses:** Complex for new players, dated visuals in some areas
- **Our Opportunity:** Simpler, more accessible, cuter aesthetic

#### 2. **Fishing Empire Simulator**
- **Visits:** 50M+
- **Strengths:** Good progression loop, solid core gameplay
- **Weaknesses:** Less frequent updates, smaller community
- **Our Opportunity:** More active development, unique lobster niche

#### 3. **Ocean Tycoon**
- **Visits:** 10M+
- **Strengths:** Broad ocean theme, multiple activities
- **Weaknesses:** Unfocused gameplay, tries to do too much
- **Our Opportunity:** Focused lobster fantasy, clearer progression

### Indirect Competitors (Popular Tycoons):

#### 4. **Pet Simulator X / 99**
- **Why Relevant:** Dominant collector tycoon, sets player expectations
- **Lessons:** Regular events, exciting rare drops, trading economy
- **Adapt:** Event structure, rarity tiers, trading system

#### 5. **Bee Swarm Simulator**
- **Why Relevant:** Animal-based collection tycoon with massive success
- **Lessons:** Deep progression, unique theme, long-term retention
- **Adapt:** Depth of content, creature variety, quest systems

#### 6. **My Restaurant**
- **Why Relevant:** Business tycoon with similar "grow your empire" loop
- **Lessons:** Satisfying upgrade visuals, employee/automation systems
- **Adapt:** Visual feedback for upgrades, automation gamepasses
