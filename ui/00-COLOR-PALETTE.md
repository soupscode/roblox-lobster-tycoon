## Design Principles

1. **Chaos with Structure**: Information is organized, but visually playful
2. **Always Slightly Animated**: Idle breathing, bobbing, wobbling
3. **Bubble Everything**: Buttons should feel squishy, pop-able
4. **Lobster Claws as Cursors**: On hover, show claw pinch animations
5. **Ocean Particles**: Constant floating bubbles, seaweed bits in backgrounds
6. **Comic Sans Energy (But Not Actually Comic Sans)**: Fun, not serious

---

## Animation Style Guide

### Button Interactions
- **Hover**: Scale 1.05x, glow effect, slight rotation (2-3Â°)
- **Click**: Squash down to 0.95x, then bounce back to 1.1x
- **Success**: Bubble burst effect, confetti of small lobsters

### Transitions
- **Screen Wipes**: Water splash/wave effect
- **Menu Opens**: Bubble float-in from bottom
- **Menu Closes**: Pop and dissolve into bubbles

### Idle Animations
- **Money Counter**: Gentle pulse every 2s
- **Lobster Icons**: Slow rotate + bob (offset timing for chaos)
- **Background**: Parallax seaweed sway, bubble float upward

---

## UI Shapes & Borders

- **Rounded Corners**: Minimum 12px, maximum 24px
- **Button Style**: Soft shadows, 3D effect (not flat)
- **Card Style**: Floating panels with drop shadow
- **Border Thickness**: 3-4px for emphasis, 2px for subtle

---

## Sound Design Notes (For UI Implementation)

- **Button Click**: Wet "pop" or bubble burst
- **Purchase**: Cash register + claw snap
- **Rare Drop**: Sparkle + bubbles + suspenseful water whoosh
- **Rebirth**: Ascending water vortex sound (5 seconds)

---

## Mobile Considerations

- **Minimum Touch Target**: 60x60px (thumb-friendly)
- **Spacing**: 16px minimum between interactive elements
- **Text Size**: Minimum 14pt for body, 24pt for important numbers
- **Gesture Support**: Swipe to close menus, pinch to zoom inventory
